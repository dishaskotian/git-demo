The sun climbs up to meet the day,
Across the fields of gold and green.
The morning mist will fade away,
On paths where we have always been.

The river hums a steady tune,
It carves a line through ancient stone.
Beneath the high and silent moon,
We find a strength we’ve always known.

The stars begin to find their place,
As evening shadows softly fall.
With quiet hope and steady grace,
We find the rhythm of it all.
